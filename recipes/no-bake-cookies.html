No Bake Cookies


Home

Description

No Bake Cookies are a type of cookie that requires no oven time. They are typically made by combining ingredients such as oats, cocoa powder, butter or margarine, sugar, milk, and sometimes peanut butter.

The mixture is brought to a boil on the stove, then dropped by spoonfuls onto waxed paper or parchment to cool and set. The cookies are known for their quick preparation and chewy texture, making them a popular choice for homemade treats.

Ingredients :

  • 3 cups quick oats
  • 1 cup sweetened flaked coconut
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter

Directions

  1. Line a baking sheet with waxed paper.
  2. Mix oats & coconut in a lrg bowl until thoroughly combined.
  3. Stir sugar, cocoa powder, milk, and butter together in a saucepan over medium heat until smooth. Bring to a boil and cook, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes. Pour over oats and coconut; mix quickly to coat.
  4. Drop tablespoonfuls onto the prepared baking sheet. Let cookies cool & harden;store in an airtight container.
